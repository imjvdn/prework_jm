Mac Miller Tribute Page!

Bio

Mac Miller was born on January 19, 1992 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, as Malcolm James McCormick. He was an actor, composer, friend and an inspiration to so many people. He tragically died on September 7, 2018 in San Fernando Valley, California.
